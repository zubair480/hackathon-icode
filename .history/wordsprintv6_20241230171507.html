WordSprint Hackathon 6.0 Details

Location

Join us in breaking barriers and fostering innovation at our remote hackathon! Regardless of where you are, everyone is welcome to contribute their unique perspectives and skills. Let's come together as a global community to collaborate, learn, and create meaningful projects that make a difference.

Event Timing

Mark your calendars! Our exciting hackathon kicks off on 27th December 2024, Friday(9:00 PM PKT), and runs until 30th December 2024, Monday(9:00 PM PKT). Get ready to dive into a whirlwind of innovation and collaboration as we embark on this exciting journey together.

Team Formation and Registration

Create teams of 1 to 3 members. Designate one member to register all team members via our Google Forms link. Need teammates? Visit our Discord server to connect with others. Watch for further instructions via email after registration.

Judging Criteria

Throughout the evaluation process, your project will be judged on slides, video demo, prototype functionality, Git usage, code quality, and requirement coverage. Aim for excellence in each area to maximize your project's success. Good luck!
